Projects

Plymouth Energy Community Damp and Mould Application (C#)

What did I do?

  • UI Design and creation within the Unity Game Engine
  • Lead scrums as the project lead/product owner
  • Code Review for the team using Git

What was it?

The Damp and Mould application was a project developed within the Unity engine, given to us by Plymouth Energy Community, in order to make it easier to get information to homeowners who may be struggling with damp and mould within their home. It was meant to be used as an aid while visiting these people, who may learn better from a visual representation of the issues that their actions or inactions may cause. Colours and fonts were reused from the clients own website, to provide a fluid and consistent approach.

Dhelve (C#)

  • Carried out user testing
  • Created supporting documents - Standard Operating Procedures, Technical Document, Design Document, Risk Assessment
